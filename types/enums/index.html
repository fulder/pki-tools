RevokeMode ¶

Bases: Enum

Specifies in what precedence and protocols (OCSP, CRL) to check revocation status of a certificate

Attributes:
  • OCSP_FALLBACK_CRL –

    Check OCSP first and fallback to CRL if it fails

  • OCSP_ONLY –

    Only check OCSP and ignore CRL

  • CRL_ONLY –

    Only check CRL and ignore OCSP
